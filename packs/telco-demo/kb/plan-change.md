# Changing your mobile plan

You can change your plan at any time. The effect of the change depends on
whether you are moving up or down:

- **Upgrade** (more data, more minutes, or a richer feature set) — applies
  immediately. We prorate the difference for the remainder of the current
  cycle and add it to the next invoice.
- **Downgrade** (less data or fewer features) — applies on the next
  billing cycle start. This protects the allowance you already paid for
  in the current month.
- **Add-on** (extra international minutes, roaming pass, device
  protection) — applies immediately and runs for the term you selected.

Changes to a plan that is part of a fixed-term contract may carry an
early-termination fee. The fee, if any, is shown before the change is
confirmed.

You can preview the effect of a change before committing. If you are
inside the cooling-off window of a fresh contract, you can revert a plan
change once at no charge.
