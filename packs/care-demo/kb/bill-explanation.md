# Understanding your monthly bill

Your monthly bill is split into four sections so the charges are easy to
audit:

- **Plan charges** — the recurring price of your subscribed plan,
  prorated when the plan changed mid-cycle.
- **Usage charges** — anything outside your plan's allowance: overage
  on capacity, premium add-ons, or pay-as-you-go items. Each line shows
  the date, the item, the quantity or volume, and the rate applied.
- **One-off charges** — equipment instalments, accessory purchases, late
  payment fees, or replacement fees.
- **Credits and adjustments** — refunds, goodwill credits, loyalty
  discounts. These reduce the amount due.

The **Amount due** at the top of the invoice is the sum of all four
sections after taxes. If your invoice is materially higher than the
previous month, the most common reason is a one-off usage spike that
the assistant can break down line by line.

If you believe a specific line item is wrong, contact support with the
line item label and the date. A care agent can confirm the rating and,
where the charge is in error, issue a credit on the spot.
